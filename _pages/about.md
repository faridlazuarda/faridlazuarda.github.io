I am a PhD student at the [University of Edinburgh](https://www.ed.ac.uk/), advised by [Edoardo Ponti](https://ducdauge.github.io/) and [Alexandra Birch](https://sites.google.com/view/alexandra-birch/). I am a member of the [Institute for Language, Cognition and Computation (ILCC)](https://informatics.ed.ac.uk/ilcc) and the [Edinburgh NLP Group](https://edinburghnlp.inf.ed.ac.uk/).

My research focuses on model memory, efficient inference, and evaluation for foundation models.

I am currently building a culturally grounded video/world-model benchmark.

In parallel, I work on memory compression for long-context language models.

More broadly, I am interested in how models preserve, compress, and use context over long horizons.

Before Edinburgh, I was a Research Assistant at [MBZUAI](https://mbzuai.ac.ae/) in Abu Dhabi and a visiting researcher at the [UKP Lab, TU Darmstadt](https://www.informatik.tu-darmstadt.de/ukp), where I worked on multilingual, multicultural, and parameter-efficient NLP.
