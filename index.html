Hi, I'm Farid!

I am a researcher in the NLP Department at MBZUAI, Abu Dhabi, under the supervision of Dr. Alham Fikri Aji. My work focuses on efficient learning, multilinguality, and multicultural adaptation for large language models (LLMs). I am collaborating with Prof. Monojit Choudhury to systematically survey and evaluate current research on cultural aspects in LLMs. Additionally, during my internship at UKP Lab, TU Darmstadt, I am working with Prof. Iryna Gurevych on a study focused on Cultural Value Adaptation using Parameter-Efficient Modules in LLMs.
